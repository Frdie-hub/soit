Who are we?

We are the School of Information Technology, our School offers a Two(2) years course in associate of Information Technology,
And a Two(2) years course in Bachelor of Information Technology majoring in Digital Design or Application Programming,
We also give opportunities for students to recieve certificates equal that of the national certificate given by the government of the Philippines.

BSIT Specializations

Digital Design

Enhance your creativity with our course In Digital Design, here you can master photoshop, enhance your Web Designing Skills with you mastery of CSS, Java Script, HTML and more!

Application Programming

Do you like games, browsing through social media or just watching videos in you favorite streaming app, then it wont be long for you to create one after you enroll to our Course in Application Programming, here you will master the likes of Java, Php, C++ and more!
